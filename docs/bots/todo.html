📋 Todo Bot

Intelligent Discord bot that automatically converts channel messages into structured todos with smart parsing of priority, dates, and family members. Features comprehensive Todoist integration and advanced task management capabilities.

🟢 LIVE & OPERATIONAL 🤖 Smart Parsing 📊 Todoist API 🎯 Priority Detection

🎯 Why This Bot Exists

Managing todos manually is time-consuming and often leads to forgotten tasks. The Todo Bot streamlines task management by:

  • 🤖 Automatic Todo Creation: Every Discord message becomes a structured todo automatically
  • 🧠 Smart Parsing: Intelligently detects priority, dates, and family members from natural language
  • 📊 Todoist Integration: Seamlessly syncs with Todoist for comprehensive task management
  • 🎯 Priority Detection: Automatically assigns priority levels based on keywords
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Labels: Smart detection of family members for task assignment
