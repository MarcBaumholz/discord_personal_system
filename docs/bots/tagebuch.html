📔 Tagebuch Bot

Intelligent Discord bot that automatically saves journal entries to Notion with smart title generation, daily reminders at 22:00, and beautiful German language support for personal reflection and growth.

🟢 LIVE & OPERATIONAL 📝 Smart Titles ⏰ Daily Reminders 🇩🇪 German Language

🎯 Why This Bot Exists

Keeping a journal manually is often forgotten or becomes a chore. The Tagebuch Bot makes journaling effortless by:

  • 📝 Automatic Saving: Simply write in Discord and it's automatically saved to Notion
  • 🧠 Smart Titles: AI generates meaningful titles from your journal text
  • ⏰ Daily Reminders: Gentle reminders at 22:00 to encourage daily reflection
  • 🇩🇪 German Language: Designed specifically for German journal entries
  • 💾 Organized Storage: All entries neatly organized in Notion with dates and titles

⏰ Daily Reminder System

🕙 22:00 Daily Reminder

Every day at 22:00 (Europe/Berlin timezone), the bot sends a gentle reminder:

📔 Tagebuch-Erinnerung

Zeit für deinen täglichen Tagebucheintrag!

  • 💭 Heute reflektieren: Wie war dein Tag?
  • 🎯 Was hast du erlebt? Besondere Momente festhalten
  • 🙏 Wofür bist du dankbar? Dankbarkeit üben

💾 Notion Database Structure

Titel (Title) - Auto-generated meaningful title from journal text
Datum (Date) - Date when the journal entry was created
Text (Rich Text) - Full journal entry text content
