🍽️ Calories Bot

Intelligent Discord bot that analyzes food images using AI vision, estimates calories with confidence scoring, and automatically saves everything to Notion. Features automated monthly reports with beautiful charts and personalized insights.

🟢 LIVE & OPERATIONAL 📊 Monthly Reports 🌍 Multi-Language 📱 Mobile Ready

🎯 Why This Bot Exists

Tracking calories manually is tedious and often inaccurate. The Calories Bot solves this by:

  • 🤖 Instant Analysis: Upload any food image and get calorie estimates in seconds
  • 📊 Automatic Tracking: No more manual logging - everything goes straight to Notion
  • 📈 Visual Insights: Monthly reports with charts show your eating patterns
  • 🎯 Confidence Scoring: Know how accurate each estimate is
  • 🌍 Language Support: Works with German and English food descriptions

🔧 Technical Stack

🤖 AI & Vision

  • OpenRouter API: Free qwen/qwen2-vl-7b-instruct model
  • Vision Analysis: Food recognition and portion estimation
  • Confidence Scoring: 0-100% accuracy indicators

💾 Data & Storage

  • Notion API: Automatic database integration
  • Database ID: 20ed42a1faf5807497c2f350ff84ea8d
  • Schema: Food, Calories, Date, Person, Confidence, Picture

📊 Analytics & Reports

  • Matplotlib: Chart generation for monthly reports
  • Pandas: Data processing and analysis
  • Schedule: Automated monthly report generation
