Notion • Todoist

Meal Plan Bot

Generates a weekly meal plan from Notion recipes and creates a Todoist shopping list. Triggered via 👍 reaction in the Erinnerungen channel.

What it does

Notion Recipes

Fetches recipes and selects 3 for weekly planning.

Shopping List

Extracts items and adds them to Todoist Einkaufsliste project.

Reactions

List generation triggered by 👍 reaction in the Erinnerungen channel.

Channel: 1361083869729919046

Short use cases

  • React with 👍 on Sunday → receive plan + shopping list.
  • Open Todoist to review automatically added items.
  • Change recipes in Notion to influence upcoming plans.
